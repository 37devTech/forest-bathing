湘南の地元の森をガイド付きで巡り、五感を高めて自然とのつながりを深めるためのエクササイズやアクティビティをご紹介します。ツアー中は、地元の植物や木々の健康効果や健康効果に関する情報も提供されます。

I will take you on a guided tour of a local forest in Shonan and walk you through exercises and activities to enhance your senses and help you become more connected to nature. During the tour you will be provided in information about the health benefits of local plants, trees, and their health properties.
